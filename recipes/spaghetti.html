Spaghetti oh so good

Description

Spaghetti is a simple recipe. Begin by boiling water and salt the devil out of it. Once it has begun boiling, place the pasta in until al dente. Drain pasta water, keep about a cup for the sauce. I use canned sauce so I don't. Then mix the canned cause in with the spaghetti and voila. Dinner is served.

Ingredients

  • Boiling Water
  • Noods
  • Sauce from your favorite can
  • About 20 minutes
  1. Place enough water to cover noodles while cooking.
  2. Salt the water to your desired taste.
  3. Place noodles into the water once it is boiling.
  4. Once the noodles are cooked al dente, drain the water. Save some water if you like your sauce a little more watery.
  5. Open the jar of sauce and mix it in well.
  6. Enjoy!
