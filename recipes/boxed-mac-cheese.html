The Best Mac and Cheese

Description

Parents and children everywhere love Kraft. Don't tell your friends though, they may mock you for enjoying it.

Ingredients

  • Pot with a lid.
  • Box of Kraft Mac and Cheese.
  • Water.
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Patience

Directions

  1. Obtain an appropriate sized pan and fill it with enough water to cover the noodles from the box.
  2. Then, salt the water to taste and bring the water to a boil.
  3. Once the water is boiling, place the noodles from the box into the water.
  4. Allow the noodles to cook until desired.
  5. Using a collander, drain the water from the pot and replace the noodles into the pot.
  6. Place an 8th of a stick of butter and a tablespoon of milk into the pot and mix.
  7. Finally, mix the powdered cheese in. If the cheese is too thick, add a little more milk.
  8. Enjoy!
